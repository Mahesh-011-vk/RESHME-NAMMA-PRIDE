# RESHME – NAMMA PRIDE

An AI-powered Android application developed using Kotlin and GenAI to support sericulture farmers by monitoring silkworm rearing conditions and providing smart climate guidance.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

Karnataka is known as the silk capital of India, where silkworm farming plays a major role in agriculture and rural livelihoods. Silkworms are highly sensitive to environmental conditions such as temperature and humidity. Even a small variation can affect cocoon quality and farmer profits.

RESHME – NAMMA PRIDE acts as a digital “Sericulture Guard” that helps farmers manually track environmental conditions and receive intelligent recommendations for maintaining ideal silkworm growth conditions.

---

## 🎯 Objective

The main objective of this project is to help small-scale sericulture farmers monitor and manage silkworm rearing environments without needing expensive IoT sensors or advanced equipment.

The application provides:
- Climate monitoring
- Batch tracking
- Smart AI suggestions
- Rearing guidance
- Harvest reminders

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 🌡 Climate Monitoring
Farmers can enter temperature and humidity values multiple times a day to monitor the rearing house environment.

### 🐛 Batch Tracking
Allows users to create and manage silkworm batches with breed type, start date, and stage information.

### 🤖 AI Smart Suggestions
The application compares entered values with ideal silkworm growth conditions and provides recommendations.

Example:
- “Temperature is high. Open windows and maintain ventilation.”
- “Humidity is low. Sprinkle water near trays.”

### ⏰ Harvest Timer
Provides reminders for cocoon spinning and harvesting stages.

### 📊 Data Management
Stores and tracks climate records and batch details for better monitoring.

## 📱 Application Workflow

1. User creates a new silkworm batch.
2. Farmer enters daily temperature and humidity.
3. Application analyzes the values.
4. AI module compares with ideal instar conditions.
5. Smart suggestions are displayed.
6. User receives harvest and stage reminders.
